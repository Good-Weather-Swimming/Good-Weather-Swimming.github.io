Salt, Sun, and Community

Established in 2025, we are an Open Group based at the beautiful Coral Bay beach in Peyia. Our mission is simple: to bring together people who love the sea.

Whether you're a local resident or just visiting Paphos for the week, you're welcome to dive in with us. We use our WhatsApp group to coordinate swims based on the best weather conditions.

Totally Free

No membership fees or hidden costs. We swim for the joy of it.

Weather Based

We monitor the waves and wind to find the perfect swimming windows.

Swim Schedule

Our calendar is automatically updated using weather data to identify the best windows of good weather for swimming at Coral Bay. Join our WhatsApp group for real-time announcements.

Don't Swim Alone!

Join our WhatsApp group to see when the next group is heading into the water. We share meeting points, times, and current water conditions at Coral Bay.

Group Rules

  • Respect the sea and each other
  • All levels are welcome
  • Swim at your own risk
  • No spamming or advertising
